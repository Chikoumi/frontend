Neutrinet

A Belgian associative network ISP, by its users for its users

principles

The association aims at maintaining, developing and promoting the use of information exchange networks, in accordance with the principle of network neutrality and defense of fundamental human rights, notably the rights to freedom of speech, access to information and respect for privacy by supporting and empowering both individuals and communities.

The No-Name-yet-self-hosting-Box

Your own piece of Internet

We are working hard right now to provide you with a plug'n'play and easy to use self hosting server where you'll be able to manage your emails, your websites and many others while keeping all your personal data on your own box at home.

Everything is done using freesoftware like YUNOHOST, Debian and the open hardware box Olimex

How to become a member

First of all: thanks!

Becoming a member of Neutrinet is actually very simple: give your subscription (25€ or more) either:

  • to a member of administrative council at a meeting
  • to our bank by wire transfer - see the information on the right - us by email.

Bank details:

Bank: RecordBank
Name: Neutrinet ASBL
Communication: costisation of ... IBAN: BE52 6528 3497 8409
BIC: HBKABE22

Belgium law requires us to ask you for you first name, last name and home address.

While we are not expecting spontaneous unknown new members, it is recommended that before choosing to become a member of the Neutrinet ASBL/VZW you spend a bit of time discovering what we are doing and in the same letting us knowing you a bit better :)

It is not mandatory to be a member of the ASBL/VZW to contribute and participate in the life of Neutrinet but this is a nice way to support us. It is mandatory if you want to subscribe to one of our services.

If you have subscribe to the VPN service, you are already a member and don't need to pay your subscription.
